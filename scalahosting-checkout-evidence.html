What this proves — and what it does not

It proves: the captured ScalaHosting plan names, the 12-month term, SPanel selection, first-invoice totals, displayed regular monthly prices, and the checkout notice that the special initial price applies to the first invoice only.

It does not prove: a separate final renewal invoice, future prices, another data center, another currency, another term, independent speed, uptime, dashboard quality or support response.

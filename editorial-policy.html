Editorial policy

How Shellz creates, updates, and monetizes web hosting reviews.

Last updated: June 2026

Home › Editorial Policy

Our goal

Shellz exists to help beginners and small website operators compare hosting plans without relying only on advertised discounts. Our pages are designed to explain buyer fit, plan limits, renewal pricing, support, backups, SSL, email, and upgrade paths.

How we rank providers

Ranking positions consider beginner usability, public plan features, pricing clarity, WordPress tools, support availability, backups, refund policy, upgrade path, and coupon transparency. Affiliate commissions may be available, but they should not be the only reason a provider appears on a list.

Affiliate relationship

Some links are affiliate links and use rel="sponsored nofollow". If a visitor buys through those links, Shellz may earn a commission at no extra cost to the visitor.

Updates

Hosting features and coupons change often. We aim to update commercial pages regularly and mark pages with a visible “Last updated” date. Visitors should verify final details on the provider checkout page.

Evidence policy

The strongest reviews should include screenshots, speed tests, uptime checks, support response notes, checkout screenshots, and renewal-price verification. Shellz adds these assets when hands-on testing is available, so readers can separate hands-on results from provider-published claims.

Shellz helps hosting buyers compare exact plan examples, renewal risk, coupon status, and published proof before paying.

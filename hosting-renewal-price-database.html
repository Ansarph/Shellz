Why exact plan names matter

Providers can have shared, WordPress, VPS, cloud, and managed products with different renewal models. “Hostinger renewal” or “Bluehost renewal” is too broad without the plan and term.

Why 3-year cost is blank

Shellz only calculates a multi-year estimate when the exact first-term length, full invoice logic, renewal term, and currency are present. Missing inputs remain “not yet calculated.”

How to verify a row

Open the provider source, match the exact plan, record the term and amount due, then compare the renewal line for the same product before payment.

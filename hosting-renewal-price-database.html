How Shellz reads renewal prices

  • Intro price: the discounted rate shown before checkout, usually tied to a 12, 24, 36, or 48-month term.
  • Renewal price: the price after the first promotional term ends. This is the number to compare for long-term value.
  • Real cost: hosting renewal plus domain renewal, email, backups, security, migration, taxes, and optional add-ons.
  • Verification rule: provider prices and currencies can change by region, so save a screenshot of the checkout page before paying.

Buyer answers from the evidence

What the comparison can—and cannot—tell you

Lowest first invoice

Use the captured total only for the exact plan and term shown. A low first bill does not mean the lowest renewal or best feature set.

Lowest commitment

A monthly plan reduces upfront commitment, but the next charge can arrive quickly and the effective discount may apply only once.

Biggest renewal jump

Percentage jumps are calculated from monthly equivalents. Regular-price estimates are not presented as confirmed future invoices.

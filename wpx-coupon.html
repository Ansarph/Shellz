Before you use this deal

  • Choose monthly when: you want a lower first payment and are still deciding whether WPX suits your site.
  • Choose annual when: you already expect to stay for a year and the larger upfront payment fits your budget.
  • Compare the next charge: our monthly check moved from $3.78 due today to a $17.99 next monthly charge; the annual check showed $161.91 due today and a $179.90 next one-year charge.
  • Check your own cart: coupon outcomes can differ by plan, term, account, country, provider rules, and date.

How to verify the WPX coupon/deal

  1. Open WPX and select the exact WordPress plan you actually want.
  2. Choose monthly billing if you are checking SAVE79, or an annual term if you are checking BPWPX.
  3. Apply the code only where the WPX checkout accepts it and confirms a promo code is active.
  4. Compare the visible saving, Total Due Today, and next monthly or annual charge before payment.
  5. Keep a screenshot of your own checkout because the result can change by plan, term, account, country, or date.

Is there an official public coupon code?

Zalvis's documentation says customers will see a coupon field at checkout and that active coupon codes can be entered there. It also says the company is currently offering 50% off hosting plans. However, that public article does not display the actual reusable code text.

Zalvis may support custom or campaign-specific coupon codes, but Shellz does not publish a reusable code unless it is visible on an official Zalvis page or verified at checkout. Use the current official deal link and confirm the live discount before buying.

How to claim the official deals

  1. Open the Zalvis affiliate link.
  2. Select a WordPress, WooCommerce, or eligible hosting plan.
  3. Compare monthly and annual billing.
  4. Select annual billing to check the two-month-free promotion.
  5. Look for an automatically applied 50% promotional price.
  6. Use the coupon field only with a code shown by Zalvis or provided through an official promotion.
  7. Review the renewal price before payment.
  8. Save the order summary and refund terms.

Important pricing warning

The WordPress Essential plan is currently advertised at $29.90 for the first year and $149.50 at renewal. Professional is $49.90 initially and $249.50 at renewal; Business is $99.90 initially and $499.50 at renewal. The discount is significant, but so is the renewal difference.

Buyer safety checklist

Before you use this coupon or deal

Use this page as a buying checkpoint before paying for Zalvis. A discount is only useful if the final checkout total, renewal price, plan limits, and refund rules still fit your project.

  • Deal type: Confirm whether this is a typed coupon code, an auto-applied offer, a voucher, or a normal public promotion.
  • Final price: Check the cart total after the discount, including billing term, taxes, setup fees, domain renewal, email, backups, and security add-ons.
  • Renewal warning: Most hosting discounts apply to the first term only. Save the renewal price before paying.
  • Plan fit: Do not choose a larger or longer plan only because the discount looks bigger.
  • Proof check: Open the official provider checkout page and confirm the offer still appears before entering payment details.

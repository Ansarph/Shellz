Editor note

My quick answer

ScalaHosting is the better first click for most readers comparing these two options.

ScalaHosting is the easier starting point for many VPS-curious users because the offer is simpler to understand. Cloudways is better when you specifically want managed cloud flexibility and you are comfortable choosing the underlying cloud provider.

Important: this page is intentionally honest about proof. Shellz has not added checkout screenshots, renewal screenshots, speed-test screenshots, or uptime data for this comparison yet. Those are marked as not yet published instead of being claimed.

How I would think about this decision

The important question is not simply which platform is stronger. It is whether you want a guided managed VPS experience or a managed cloud layer where you still need to understand provider, server size, and usage decisions.

Cloudways can be powerful, but it can also feel abstract for a beginner. ScalaHosting is often easier to explain to a small business owner who wants managed VPS-style hosting without thinking like a cloud engineer.

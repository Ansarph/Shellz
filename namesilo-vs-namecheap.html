Quick verdict

Choose NameSilo for clean domain separation; choose Namecheap for strong promos and a wider product ecosystem.

NameSilo is a good fit when you want the domain to stay separate from hosting and you want to test Shellz reader codes. Namecheap is attractive when you want visible first-year domain promos, free privacy on eligible domains, and more add-on services in one account.

FAQs

Common questions before you buy

Is NameSilo or Namecheap cheaper for .com domains?

It changes by promotion, customer eligibility, and renewal year. Check the live first-year price and renewal price before choosing.

Which registrar is better for beginners?

Namecheap may feel more familiar and promotional. NameSilo can feel simpler if you want a domain-focused registrar without mixing hosting into the same decision.

Can I use a NameSilo or Namecheap domain with any host?

Yes. You can usually point the domain to another host using nameservers or DNS records.

Do NameSilo codes always work?

No. Coupon acceptance can vary by TLD, cart type, and checkout rules. Try one code at a time and compare the final total.

Domain + hosting decision

Should you bundle domain and hosting or buy separately?

For a first website, the easiest setup is usually one provider for hosting and domain. For more control, keep the domain at a registrar and connect it to the host. Compare the first bill, renewal bill, coupon rules, and how easy it will be to move later.

Price and renewal

The first bill and the second bill are two different buying decisions

Shellz does not treat a promotional monthly number as the long-term cost. Before paying for Namecheap, save the plan name, billing term, full amount due today, renewal amount, renewal billing cycle, domain renewal, email cost or limits, backup rules, and optional add-ons.

  • 1. First totalRecord the actual amount charged today, not only the advertised monthly equivalent.
  • 2. Renewal amountCheck the price for the same plan after the promotional or first billing period.
  • 3. Included servicesSeparate domain, email, backups, security, migration, and premium support from the hosting price.
  • 4. Exit costKnow the refund terms, migration path, and what happens to a bundled domain or email service if you leave.

Privacy Policy

Shellz respects visitor privacy. This page explains the basic information Shellz may collect when visitors use shellz.com, click provider links, read hosting reviews, or contact the site.

Shellz is not responsible for how third-party websites collect or use information after you leave shellz.com.

Local browser storage

Shellz may use your browser’s local storage to remember recent affiliate-link interactions on your own device. For example, the site script may store limited click-history details such as the provider name, page placement, destination URL, and timestamp under a browser storage key such as shellz_affiliate_clicks. This helps Shellz keep coupon and provider-button behavior consistent inside the browser. This information is stored locally in your browser and can be cleared by deleting site data in your browser settings.

Cookies and analytics

Shellz may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics, affiliate attribution, site functionality, or performance measurement. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. If Shellz adds advertising or analytics tools in the future, those tools may set their own cookies or collect aggregated usage information according to their own policies.

External links

Shellz links to external websites for hosting plans, official provider information, coupon policies, and source references. External websites may change their prices, offers, privacy practices, or terms without notice. Always review the provider’s official page before buying.

Children’s privacy

Shellz is intended for general website visitors and hosting buyers. It is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

Shellz may update this privacy policy when site features, analytics tools, affiliate programs, or legal requirements change. The latest version will be published on this page.

What this hub proves — and what it does not

It proves: the captured plan, term, discount/promo state, total today and renewal note or regular-price notice shown in the dated cart screenshot.

It does not prove: future prices, other countries, other currencies, other plans, uptime, speed, support quality, or that the same coupon or initial offer will remain available.

SPanel vs Hostinger VPS tools

These are different approaches to making VPS management easier

ScalaHosting positions SPanel as an all-in-one hosting management platform for cloud VPS services, covering website tasks such as domains, email, files, and databases.

Hostinger's VPS is still described as self-managed, but the company provides a dashboard, pre-made templates, tutorials, step-by-step guides, and AI assistance that can guide or perform various VPS-related tasks.

The distinction matters: tools can reduce friction, but they do not automatically make the underlying service a fully managed VPS. Compare the support and management scope of the exact plan before buying.

Choose the Scala routeWhen managed administration is part of the value you are buying.
Choose the Hostinger VPS routeWhen you accept self-management and want tools that make those tasks easier.

Cost comparison

Do not compare a managed plan with a self-managed plan by headline price alone

A fair cost comparison needs the same CPU/RAM class, storage, billing term, renewal amount, backup needs, control panel, and management responsibility. If one service includes more management, the raw monthly price is not an apples-to-apples comparison.

FAQ

ScalaHosting vs Hostinger VPS questions

Is ScalaHosting managed while Hostinger VPS is self-managed?

ScalaHosting currently offers a managed cloud hosting route. Hostinger describes its VPS service as self-managed, with a dashboard, templates, learning resources, and AI assistance to make server tasks easier.

Which is better for a VPS beginner?

A beginner who does not want to manage server administration should first compare ScalaHosting’s managed route. A beginner willing to learn server management may prefer Hostinger VPS tools and its self-managed model.

Does SPanel make ScalaHosting easier?

SPanel centralizes cloud VPS hosting tasks such as domains, email, files, and databases. Whether it is easier depends on your existing workflow and whether you prefer its approach over other panels.

Which provider is cheaper?

Do not assume one is always cheaper. Compare the exact plan, resources, billing term, renewal amount, management scope, and extras at the time you buy.

Price and renewal

The first bill and the second bill are two different buying decisions

Shellz does not treat a promotional monthly number as the long-term cost. Before paying for Bluehost, save the plan name, billing term, full amount due today, renewal amount, renewal billing cycle, domain renewal, email cost or limits, backup rules, and optional add-ons.

  • 1. First totalRecord the actual amount charged today, not only the advertised monthly equivalent.
  • 2. Renewal amountCheck the price for the same plan after the promotional or first billing period.
  • 3. Included servicesSeparate domain, email, backups, security, migration, and premium support from the hosting price.
  • 4. Exit costKnow the refund terms, migration path, and what happens to a bundled domain or email service if you leave.

Support scope

Ask what support covers before you need urgent help

Check the current support channels and what the team is responsible for. Hosting support may help with the platform while plugin, theme, code, or application issues can fall outside normal scope. Shellz has not yet published a timed Bluehost support test, so the review does not assign a support score.

Hosting-platform problem

Ask whether account, server, DNS, SSL, backup, or platform issues are within normal support scope.

WordPress or application problem

Check whether plugins, themes, custom code, database errors, or application debugging are included or excluded.

Response expectations

What this proves — and what it does not

It proves: the captured SiteGround StartUp plan, 12-month term, USA data center, $47.88 first invoice, displayed $17.99/mo regular price, visible 78% off badge and an unselected Site Scanner add-on in the dated checkout screenshot.

It does not prove: a separate final renewal invoice, future prices, another country, another currency, another plan, uptime, speed, support quality or that the same discount will remain available later.

Add-on note: the screenshot shows Site Scanner listed as an extra service at $2.49/mo, but it was not selected in the captured order summary.

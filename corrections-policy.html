How to report an issue

If you find an outdated price, broken affiliate link, expired coupon, inaccurate feature note, or unclear comparison, contact us through the contact page.

What we review

  • Provider pricing and renewal information
  • Coupon availability and expiration notes
  • Plan features, storage, backups, SSL, email, and support options
  • Broken links or incorrect redirects
  • Content that may be unclear or misleading

Correction process

When a correction is accepted, we update the page, adjust the last-updated date, and clarify the affected sentence or section. For major changes, we may add a note explaining what changed.

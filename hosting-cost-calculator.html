FAQ

Website cost questions beginners ask

Can I really start a website under $25?

Sometimes, yes. A simple starter website can fit under $25 if you avoid paid add-ons, choose a low-cost first term, or use free/static hosting with a domain. A full WordPress business setup may cost more.

Why does hosting renewal price comparison matter?

The first price is often promotional. Renewal is the price you may pay after the first billing term. A deal that looks cheap today may be less attractive if the second bill is much higher.

Should I buy email immediately?

Only if you need a professional inbox now. Many beginners can launch the site first, then add business email after the site structure is ready.

What is the biggest checkout mistake?

Buying too many extras before you understand the website. Start with the essentials, save the renewal details, and upgrade only when the site needs it.

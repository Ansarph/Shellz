Before you pay

Use this 5-step buying flow

  1. Pick the hosting type first. Do not start from the coupon page if you do not know whether you need shared, VPS, cloud, or WordPress hosting.
  2. Check the first bill and renewal bill. The intro price can be much lower than the renewal price.
  3. Check what is included. Look for domain, SSL, email, backups, malware protection, staging, and migration support.
  4. Read the refund rules. Money-back guarantees may exclude domains, add-ons, or renewal payments.
  5. Apply coupons last. A coupon is useful only after you know the plan fits your project.

FAQ

Web Hosting Finder questions

Is this finder a replacement for a full review?

No. It is a starting point. Use the result to open the right Shellz guide, then check price, renewal terms, refund rules, support, backups, and coupons before buying.

Should beginners avoid VPS hosting?

Most beginners should avoid unmanaged VPS hosting unless they are comfortable with server setup, security, updates, and troubleshooting. Managed VPS or beginner shared hosting is easier.

What is the safest path for a small business website?

A small business usually needs reliable shared or WordPress hosting, SSL, email options, backups, support, and clear renewal pricing. Avoid choosing only by the lowest intro price.

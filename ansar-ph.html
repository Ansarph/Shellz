How I review a hosting page

  1. Pull the live plan page. Intro price, renewal price, billing term length, free domain Y/N, free SSL Y/N, included email, included backups, included CDN.
  2. Pull the renewal page separately. If the renewal price isn't visible without logging in, I mark the provider "renewal unclear" and drop its score by 0.3.
  3. Test the coupon. Public codes, auto-applied deals, no-code deals, and checkout-only promos all get labeled differently. Anything I can't verify in the current month gets a "check current deal" tag, not a fake number.
  4. Test the checkout. I look for: hidden setup fees, location fees, mandatory add-ons, domain privacy upsells, and "trial" products that auto-renew.
  5. Check speed & uptime proof. Where I have independent PageSpeed / GTmetrix / UptimeRobot data, I publish it. Where I don't, the page says "proof pending" — never invented numbers.
  6. Write the renewal warning first. Every review page leads with what the second bill looks like. If I can't tell you, I say so.

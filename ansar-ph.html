What I check before recommending a provider

  • Whether the offer is a public code, auto-applied deal, no-code deal, or unverified claim.
  • Whether renewal price, setup fee, location fee, email, domain, backups, and plan limits can change the total cost.
  • Whether the provider is better for beginners, WordPress users, domain buyers, VPS users, or managed hosting buyers.
  • Whether a page needs stronger proof such as checkout screenshots, uptime data, speed tests, or support screenshots.

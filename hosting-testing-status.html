What Shellz needs before calling a host fully tested

Shellz will not claim verified speed, proven uptime, or confirmed checkout savings until the relevant proof is collected.

1. Checkout proof

Final checkout total, billing term, add-ons, taxes/fees where visible, and coupon/deal behavior.

2. Renewal proof

Renewal price confirmation or provider documentation showing what changes after the first term.

3. Performance proof

Speed test, TTFB/Core Web Vitals check, and uptime monitoring over time.

4. Support proof

Support response time, quality of answer, and whether beginner questions are answered clearly.

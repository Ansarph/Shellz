Testing rules

What Shellz needs before calling evidence published

Checkout proof

A dated capture or documented cart result showing the exact plan, billing term, visible add-ons, coupon/deal behavior, and final total available to the tester.

Renewal proof

An exact plan and renewal term from provider documentation, account data, or checkout evidence. Provider-wide renewal numbers are not used.

Performance proof

A comparable test site, documented test conditions, repeatable speed/TTFB/Core Web Vitals checks, and uptime monitoring collected over time.

Support proof

A dated contact channel, response time, question asked, answer quality, and a clear note about what the test can and cannot prove.

How to read the labels

Published proof is narrower than a provider rating

A dated cart capture proves only what was visible for that plan, term, location and checkout session. It does not automatically prove future pricing or performance.

Confirmed cart line

The exact renewal or next-charge line was visible in the captured cart. Re-check it because provider prices can change.

Regular-price estimate

The checkout showed a standard monthly or annual price, and Shellz calculated the equivalent next term. It is labelled as an estimate, not a final invoice.

Public provider research

The information came from a provider page or documentation rather than a Shellz checkout capture. Region, tax and plan eligibility may differ.

Not published

Shellz does not claim an independent speed, uptime or support result until the supporting method and result are visible.

Testing rules

What Shellz needs before calling evidence published

Checkout proof

A dated capture or documented cart result showing the exact plan, billing term, visible add-ons, coupon/deal behavior, and final total available to the tester.

Renewal proof

An exact plan and renewal term from provider documentation, account data, or checkout evidence. Provider-wide renewal numbers are not used.

Performance proof

A comparable test site, documented test conditions, repeatable speed/TTFB/Core Web Vitals checks, and uptime monitoring collected over time.

Support proof

A dated contact channel, response time, question asked, answer quality, and a clear note about what the test can and cannot prove.

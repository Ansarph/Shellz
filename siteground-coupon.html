Before you use this deal

  • Best for: WordPress and small business sites where support and tooling matter more than lowest first bill
  • Watch before checkout: Look at the standard renewal price and choose term length carefully before checkout.
  • Renewal warning: compare the normal renewal price, domain renewal, email, backups, security add-ons, and billing term.
  • Coupon proof: a coupon works only when the official cart accepts it and the final total changes.

How to verify the SiteGround coupon/deal

  1. Open the official SiteGround coupon, promo, or pricing page.
  2. Select the plan and billing term you actually want.
  3. Apply No public coupon code needed only if the official checkout shows a code field or confirms the discount.
  4. Check the final price, renewal price, taxes/VAT, domain terms, email, backup, and security add-ons.
  5. Take a screenshot of the checkout total if you want proof before payment.

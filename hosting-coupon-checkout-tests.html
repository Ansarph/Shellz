How to read the proof

What our screenshots can show — and what they cannot guarantee

What the evidence can show

  • A code was accepted or displayed as applied in the captured checkout flow.
  • The product, price, discount, renewal wording, or total visible in the screenshot.
  • The date on which Shellz performed the checkout check.

What the evidence cannot guarantee

  • That a code works for every product, plan, account, or country.
  • That every visitor receives the same discount or final total.
  • That the code applies to renewals unless the checkout or provider terms explicitly show it.
  • That the result will remain unchanged in the future.

Coupon checkout test FAQ

Does a checkout screenshot guarantee a coupon will work for me?

No. It documents the result Shellz saw in one captured checkout flow on a specific date. Product, plan, account, country, billing term, or provider rules can change the result.

Did Shellz complete payment during these coupon checks?

No. The documented NameSilo and HostGator checks stopped before payment.

What does “Checkout checked” mean on Shellz?

It means Shellz has a first-hand screenshot showing the code accepted or displayed as applied in the captured provider checkout flow.

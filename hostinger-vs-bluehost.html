The better choice depends on your website. Hostinger is strongest for budget hosting and beginner WordPress sites, while Bluehost is strongest for beginner WordPress sites and small businesses.

Not by itself. Compare renewal rates, backups, support, storage, email, domain renewal, migration, and your expected traffic before choosing.
